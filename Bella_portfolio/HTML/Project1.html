About I Do Prenups

Welcome to I Do Prenups. I am currently the marketing director of this fully online prenuptial agreement service based in Colorado.

Learn more about our services and team through the galleries below.

Post 1: Mission and Values

I Do Prenups is a fully online prenuptial agreement service based in Colorado. With a commitment to excellence and innovation, we are here to guide you through the journey of protecting your interests while embracing the love that brought you together.

We value the time commitment necessary in creating the right prenuptial agreement for you.

Post 2: Meet Our Founder

Kimberly R. Willoughby is a very established and successful premier family law attorney in Colorado specializing in divorce litigation, prenuptial agreements, and estate planning since 1994. Kim has received numerous awards and distinctions throughout her career.

Post 3: Meet the Team

Meet the I Do Prenups team! Cindy Jones is our Chief Operations Officer, Olivia Hefner is one of our licensed attorneys, Ali Trevino is our Associate Attorney, Bella Willoughby is our Marketing Director, and Kim Willoughby is our founder.

Post 4: Our Services

Our services include three levels:

  • Do It Yourself Package: Fully online, no legal help. Fill out a questionnaire, provide financial disclosures, and have the prenup notarized.
  • Live Assistance Package: Knowledgeable assistance over phone or Zoom, but no legal help.
  • Premium Services Package: Legal help from licensed attorneys, available in person or over Zoom. Prices vary based on assistance needed.
